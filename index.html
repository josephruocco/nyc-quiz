NYC Neighborhood Quizzes

181 neighborhoods and 23 subway lines across Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and the Bronx. Four ways to find out how much of the city you actually know.

Fill In the Map
Type a neighborhood and it paints itself onto the city. Pick your boroughs, play free-form or one at a time, with or without a clock.
Typing · timed option
Pinpoint the Neighborhood
Every boundary is drawn, none are labeled. Click where you think the named neighborhood sits. 20 rounds, scored on how far off you were.
Clicking · 20 rounds
Pinpoint the Line
The subway network, drawn but unlabeled. Click anywhere along the train you're asked for, or switch to hard mode, where you get a station name and have to know which line serves it.
Clicking · 20 rounds
Which Train?
A station name and the whole alphabet of bullets. Pick every line that stops there, with no map to work it out from.
Bullets · 20 rounds
Name Every Neighborhood
No map. Just a blank list to fill in, borough by borough, until you run out of names.
Typing · no map
Boundaries from NYC Planning NTA data.
